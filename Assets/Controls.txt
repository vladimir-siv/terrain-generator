MouseLeft				-	Build terrain
MouseRight				-	Clear terrain
W						-	Move forward
A						-	Strafe left
S						-	Move backward
D						-	Strafe right
MouseButton_Forward		-	Fly up
MouseButton_Back		-	Fly down
MouseWheel				-	Rotate Camera
MouseWheelScroll		-	BrushZCorrection
+						-	Increase brush Radius
-						-	Decrease brush Radius
R						-	Reset brush ZCorrection & Radius
ArrowUp					-	Increase terrain granularity
ArrowDown				-	Decrease terrain granularity
G						-	Generate random complete terrain
C						-	Clear (reset) complete terrain
